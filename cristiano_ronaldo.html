Ronaldo holds the records for the most goals (130) and assists (40) in the history of the UEFA Champions League. He is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,000 professional career appearances and has scored over 700 senior career goals for club and country

Here's a timeline of Ronaldo's life:

  • Born 5 February 1985
  • Ronaldo is the most followed athlete on social media. His Instagram followers hit 200 million in 2020.
  • The Portuguese star joined Juventus in 2018 after nine years with Real Madrid. He took a pay cut with his four-year deal worth $64 million annually.
  • He has won FIFA's Player of the Year five times and is the all-time leading goal scorer in the UEFA Champions League.

Here is a list of Ronaldo's awards:

  • La Liga (2) : 2011-12 , 2016-17
  • Euro Champion : 2016
  • UEFA Champions League (5) : 2007-08, 2013-14, 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18
  • English Champion (3) : 2007, 2008, 2009
  • Italian Champion (2) : 2018-19, 2019-20
