Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Spanish club Barcelona and the Argentina national team. Here's a timeline of Messi's life:

  • Born June 24, 1987 in Rosario, Argentina
  • At age 8, recruited by Newell's Old Boys
  • Diagnosed with hormone deficiency that restricted growth
  • Parents couldn't afford to pay the several hundred dollars a month it cost for Messi's nightly growth hormone injections
  • At age 13, family uprooted and moved to Spain when FC Barcelona offered to pay medical bills
  • May 1, 2005, Messi became the youngest player to ever score for Barcelona
  • In 2009, Messi guided Barcelona to the treble, winning the Champions League, La Liga and the Spanish Super Cup. The same year, he won the Ballon d'Or
